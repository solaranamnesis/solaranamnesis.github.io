Tsari

1. Sanya LMScope adapter tare da Nikon D810; haɗa Nikon D810 da kwamfuta; buɗe da sanya digiCamControl.

2. Daidaita ƙarfin haske da lokacin hasken kamara.

3. Nemo kusurwar da ta dace na sashin sirara tare da Live View na digiCamControl.

4. Nemo farkon focus mai dacewa (ba mai lanƙwasa sosai, wasu abubuwa suna cikin focus) tare da Live View.

5. ɗauki hoton.

6. Juya knob na focus kaɗan, kawo abubuwa masu yawa cikin focus.

7. ɗauki hoton.

8. Maimaita matakan 6 zuwa 7 har sai duk abubuwa sun fita daga focus.

9. Tura tebur na microscope sama/ƙasa yayin da kake lura da Live View har sai wani sabon hoto ya bayyana a filin gani; tabbatar da cewa akwai haɗuwa da filin gani na baya.

10. Maimaita matakan 4 zuwa 9 har sai ka kai sama/ƙasa na sashin sirara.

11. Tura tebur na microscope hagu/ dama har sai ka samu sabon filin gani tare da haɗuwa da filayen gani na layi/ ginshiƙi na baya.

12. Maimaita matakan 4 zuwa 11 har sai ka kai bangaren da ke gaban sashin sirara.

13. ɗauki wasu hotuna na hasken polarized baƙi mai tsabta, wato ba tare da wani sashin sirara a gani ba – don yanayin da irin wannan hoto zai iya taimakawa wajen gyara nan gaba.

14. Kammala; tsabtace.

Lura game da matakan 9 da 11: tunanin shine ya ɗauki grid na hotuna da ke haɗuwa na sashin sirara gaba ɗaya don haka stitching na hannu zai yiwu.

15. Ƙirƙiri folder a kwamfuta don layi/ ginshiƙi ɗaya na grid na hoto.

16. Canza fayilolin Nikon na asali don wannan layi/ ginshiƙi zuwa TIFF kuma sanya su a cikin wannan folder.

17. A cikin folder na layi/ ginshiƙi, ƙirƙiri folders na ɗaiɗaikun don kowane tantanin halin grid na hoto, wato duk ɗaukar focus daban-daban don kowane filin gani. Ana yin wannan don amfani da fasalin sarrafa taro na Zerene Stacker.

18. Maimaita 15 zuwa 17 don kowane layi/ ginshiƙi.

19. Aika folders a matsayin taro zuwa Zerene Stacker kuma a jira har sai stacking na focus ya kammala.

20. Yanzu dole ne a sami cikakken tarin hotunan da aka ɗora don grid gaba ɗaya, an tsara su a cikin folders don kowane layi/ ginshiƙi.

21. Sarrafa hotuna a matsayin taro tare da Picture Window Pro (wannan matakin yana iya canzawa bisa ga mai daukar hoto – yi la'akari da ƙara daidaitawar fari, kaifi, gyaran lanƙwasan lens, da sauran widgets. SABUNTAWA: Na gano cewa sakamakon mafi kyau ana samu idan gyaran lanƙwasan lens an yi amfani da shi a kan hotunan kafin stacking tare da Zerene Stacker).

22. Yanzu dole ne a sami cikakken tarin hotunan stacking na focus da aka sarrafa kuma su shirye ne don stitching.

23. Shigar da layi/ ginshiƙi na hotuna zuwa GIMP. (An zaɓi stitching na hannu saboda bai yi nasarar samun software wanda zai iya stitching na atomatik na sashin sirara na meteorite ba).

24. Stitch hotuna a cikin GIMP ta amfani da kayan aikin motsi da haɗawa (move and blend tool).

25. Maimaita 23 zuwa 24 don kowane layi/ ginshiƙi.

26. Aika layukan/ ginshiƙan zuwa cloud storage don samun sauri da backup.

27. Sanya kwamfutar cloud a Amazon tare da masu sarrafa 16 da 255 GB RAM (ko kowace kwamfuta mai ƙarfi da kusan 120 GB ko fiye da RAM).

28. Sanya NoMachine a server da client.

29. Sake saukar da layukan/ ginshiƙan daga cloud storage zuwa kwamfutar cloud.

30. Buɗe GIMP a kwamfutar cloud.

31. Shigar da kowane layi/ ginshiƙi a matsayin sabon layer a GIMP.

32. Stitch kowane layi/ ginshiƙi tare da kayan aikin motsi da haɗawa.

33. Ƙara launin bango a matsayin layer.

34. Fitar da hoton ƙarshe a matsayin TIFF.

35. Rarraba hoton ƙarshe zuwa kaso ƙasa don duba web.

Lura, idan hoton ya yi girma sosai, wato ya wuce 4 GB, ba za ka iya fitar da shi a matsayin ɗan ɗan ɗaya da GIMP a matsayin TIFF ba. (Sabuntawa Yuni 2022: Sabon sigar GIMP, 2.10.32, tana tallafawa shigar da fitar da BigTIFF, don haka wannan matakin yanzu na zaɓi ne) Don haka, a GIMP dole ne ka ƙara layukan jagora na kwance/ tsaye sannan ka amfani da kayan aikin guillotine na hoto don fitar da hotuna na ɗaiɗaikun. Sannan, tare da ImageMagick yi amfani da umarni:

convert Image1.tiff Image2.tiff … -append TIFF64:Output.tiff

36. Ƙirƙiri hotunan krpano.

37. Aika zuwa Gigapan.

Lura, Gigapan ba ya son fayilolin TIFF masu girma, don haka yi amfani da ImageMagick don canza hoton daga TIFF zuwa PSB sannan a aika.

Da fatar ka sanar da ni idan akwai wani abu da bai bayyana ba.
