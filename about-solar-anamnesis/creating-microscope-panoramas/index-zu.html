Inqubo

1. Hlela i-LMScope adapter ne-Nikon D810; xhumanisa i-Nikon D810 nekhompyutha; vula bese uhlela i-digiCamControl.

2. Lungisa amandla umthombo wokukhanya nesikhathi sokubuka sekhamera.

3. Thola i-engeli efanele yengxenye ebolile usebenzisa i-Live View ye-digiCamControl.

4. Thola i-focus yokuqala efanele (ayibuthanga kakhulu, izinto ezithile zisesimweni se-focus) usebenzisa i-Live View.

5. Thatha isithombe.

6. Jikeleza i-knob ye-focus kancane, bringa izinto eziningi kwi-focus.

7. Thatha isithombe.

8. Phinda izinyathelo 6 kuya ku-7 kuze zonke izinto ziphume kwi-focus.

9. Hamba isiteshi se-microscope phezulu/ngezansi njengoba ubheka i-Live View kuze isithombe esisha sibonakale endaweni yokubuka; qinisekisa ukuthi kukhona ukufana okuncane nendawo yokubuka yangaphambilini.

10. Phinda izinyathelo 4 kuya ku-9 kuze ufike phezulu/ngezansi kwengxenye ebolile.

11. Hamba isiteshi se-microscope kwesokunxele/kwesokudla kuze uthole indawo entsha yokubuka enokufana nendawo yokubuka yangaphambilini yomugqa/ikholomu.

12. Phinda izinyathelo 4 kuya ku-11 kuze ufike ohlangothini oluhlukile lwengxenye ebolile.

13. Thatha izithombe ezimbalwa zokukhanya okumnyama okuqondile okuqinile, okungukuthi ngaphandle kwengxenye ebolile kakhulu kubonakala – uma isithombe esinjalo singaba usizo ekulungiseni esizayo.

14. Phetha; hlanza.

Qaphela mayelana nezinyathelo 9 no-11: umbono ukubamba i-grid yezithombe ezifana nengxenye ebolile yonke ukuze ukulungisa ngesandla kube yinto enokwenzeka.

15. Yakha ifolda ekhompyutheni yomugqa/ikholomu eyodwa ye-grid yesithombe.

16. Guqula amafayela e-Nikon angavuthiwe lawo mugqa/ikholomu abe yi-TIFF bese uwafaka kule folder.

17. Ngaphakathi kwefolda yomugqa/ikholomu, yakha amafolda ngamunye yiseli ngayinye ye-grid yesithombe, okungukuthi ukubamba okuhlukile kokugxila kwindawo yokubuka ngayinye. Lokhu kwenziwa ukuze kusetshenziswe izici zokuphatha i-batch ze-Zerene Stacker.

18. Phinda 15 kuya ku-17 yomugqa/ikholomu ngamunye.

19. Thumela amafolda njenge-batches ku-Zerene Stacker bese ulinda kuze i-stacking ye-focus iphele.

20. Manje kufanele kube khona isethi ephelele yezithombe ezihlanganisiwe yonke i-grid, ehlelwe ngamafolda yomugqa/ikholomu ngamunye.

21. Phatha izithombe ze-batch nge-Picture Window Pro (le nyathelo ingahlelwa ngokomthwebuli wesithombe – cabanga ukwengeza ukulinganisela okumhlophe, ukuqina, ukulungiswa kwe-curve lens, nezinye izinto. UPDATES: Ngiyabona ukuthi imiphumela engcono itholakala uma ukulungiswa kwe-curve lens kusetshenziswa ezithombeni ngaphambi kokuhlanganisa nge-Zerene Stacker).

22. Manje kufanele kube khona isethi ephelele yezithombe ezihlanganisiwe ze-focus ezihlaziyiwe futhi zilungile ukulungiswa.

23. Faka umugqa/ikholomu yezithombe ku-GIMP. (Ukulungiswa ngesandla kukhethiwe ngoba angikwazanga ukuthola isoftware engalungisa ngokuzenzakalela ngempumelelo izingxenye ezibolile ze-meteorite).

24. Lungisa izithombe ngaphakathi ku-GIMP usebenzisa ithuluzi lokuhamba nokuhlanganisa (move and blend tool).

25. Phinda 23 kuya ku-24 yomugqa/ikholomu ngamunye.

26. Ngenisa imigqa/amakholomu kwi-cloud storage ukuze kutholakale kalula futhi kube backup.

27. Hlela ikhompyutha ye-cloud ku-Amazon enama-processers angu-16 kanye ne-RAM engu-255 GB (noma ikhompyutha enamandla eni-RAM engu-120 GB noma ngaphezulu).

28. Faka i-NoMachine kwi-server ne-client.

29. Khuphula imigqa/amakholomu kusuka kwi-cloud storage kwi-khompyutha ye-cloud.

30. Vula i-GIMP ekhompyutheni ye-cloud.

31. Faka umugqa/ikholomu ngamunye njenge-layer entsha ku-GIMP.

32. Lungisa umugqa/ikholomu ngamunye ndawonye usebenzisa ithuluzi lokuhamba nokuhlanganisa.

33. Faka umbala wangemuva njenge-layer.

34. Khhipha isithombe sokugcina njenge-TIFF.

35. Yehlisa isithombe sokugcina ngephesenti eliphansi ukuze libukeke ku-web.

Qaphela, uma isithombe sikhulu kakhulu, okungukuthi sikhulu kuno-4 GB, ngeke ukwazi ukukhishwa njenge-pie eyodwa nge-GIMP njenge-TIFF. (Okuthuthukisiwe Jun 2022: Inguqulo yakamuva ye-GIMP, 2.10.32, isekela ukufaka nokukhipha i-BigTIFF, ngakho le nyathelo manje iyinketho) Ngakho-ke, ku-GIMP kufanele wengeze imigqa ehlelwe ngokuvundlile/eqondile bese usebenzisa ithuluzi le-guillotine yesithombe ukuze ukhiphe izithombe ezihlukene. Bese, usebenzisa i-ImageMagick usebenzisa umyalelo:

convert Image1.tiff Image2.tiff … -append TIFF64:Output.tiff

36. Yakha izithombe ze-krpano.

37. Ngenisa ku-Gigapan.

Qaphela, i-Gigapan ayithandi amafayela amakhulu e-TIFF, ngakho-ke sebenzisa i-ImageMagick ukuze uguqule isithombe sibe yi-TIFF ibe yi-PSB bese uyayingenisa.

Sicela ungitshele uma kukhinto engacaci.
