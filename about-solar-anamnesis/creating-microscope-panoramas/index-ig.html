Usoro

1. Tọọ LMScope adapter na Nikon D810; jikọọ Nikon D810 na kọmputa; mepee ma tọọ digiCamControl.

2. Gbanwee ike isi iyi ìhè na oge nkọwa nke igwefoto.

3. Chọta akụkụ kwesịrị ekwesị nke akụkụ dị gịrịgịrị site na iji Live View nke digiCamControl.

4. Chọta ebe mmalite kwesịrị ekwesị (adịghị mgbagwoju anya, ihe ụfọdụ nọ n'ime focus) site na iji Live View.

5. Were foto.

6. Gbagharịa knob focus ntakịrị, na-ebute ihe karịrị n'ime focus.

7. Were foto.

8. Ugha nzọụkwụ 6 ruo 7 ruo mgbe ihe niile pụtara n'ime focus.

9. Gbaga n'elu/ala nke显微镜 stage ka ị na-eleba anya na Live View ruo mgbe foto ọhụrụ pụtara n'ime ebe a na-ahụ anya; jide n'aka na e nwere ụfọdụ mkpuchi na ebe a na-ahụ anya gara aga.

10. Ugha nzọụkwụ 4 ruo 9 ruo mgbe ị ruru elu/ala nke akụkụ dị gịrịgịrị.

11. Gbaga n'aka ekpe/aka nri nke显微镜 stage ruo mgbe ị nwetara ebe a na-ahụ anya ọhụrụ nwere mkpuchi na ebe a na-ahụ anya nke ahịrị/ogologo gara aga.

12. Ugha nzọụkwụ 4 ruo 11 ruo mgbe ị ruru akụkụ nke akụkụ dị gịrịgịrị.

13. Were foto ole na ole nke ìhè polarized ojii dị ọcha, ya bụ, enweghị akụkụ dị gịrịgịrị n'anya – maka ọnọdụ ebe foto dị otú ahụ nwere ike inyere aka na nhazi n'ọdịnihu.

14. Mechie; dozie.

Nkọwa gbasara nzọụkwụ 9 na 11: echiche bụ ijide grid foto na-ekpuchi akụkụ dị gịrịgịrị dum ka stitching aka nwee ike ime.

15. Mepụta folda na kọmputa maka otu ahịrị/ogologo nke grid foto.

16. Gbanwee faịlụ Nikon raw maka ahịrị/ogologo a gaa na TIFF tinye ha n'ime folda a.

17. N'ime folda ahịrị/ogologo, mepụta folda onwe onye maka sel ọ bụla nke grid foto, ya bụ, njide focus dị iche iche niile maka ebe a na-ahụ anya ọ bụla. A na-eme nke a iji jiri njirimara nhazi batch nke Zerene Stacker.

18. Ugha 15 ruo 17 maka ahịrị/ogologo ọ bụla.

19. Zipu folda dị ka batches na Zerene Stacker ma chere ruo mgbe stacking focus gwụrụ.

20. Ugbu a, a ga-enwe set zuru oke nke foto ejikọtara maka grid dum, haziri na folda maka ahịrị/ogologo ọ bụla.

21. Nhazi foto batch na Picture Window Pro (nzọụkwụ a nwere ike ịgbanwe dabere na onye na-ese foto – tụlee itinye nha ọcha, ịdị mma, nhazi curvature lens, na widget ndị ọzọ. UPDATE: Achọpụtara m na nsonaazụ kacha mma na-abịa ma a na-etinye nhazi curvature lens na foto tupu stacking na Zerene Stacker).

22. Ugbu a, a ga-enwe set zuru oke nke foto ejikọtara focus nke ejiri nhazi mee ma ugbu a dị njikere maka stitching.

23. Banye otu ahịrị/ogologo foto na GIMP. (A họọrọ stitching aka n'ihi na m enwetaghị ngwaọrụ nke nwere ike ijikọta akụkụ dị gịrịgịrị nke meteorite n'ụzọ ziri ezi).

24. Jikọọ foto n'ime GIMP site na iji ngwaọrụ na-ebufe na ịgwakọta (move and blend tool).

25. Ugha 23 ruo 24 maka ahịrị/ogologo ọ bụla.

26. Bulite ahịrị/ogologo na nchekwa cloud maka nchọpụta ngwa ngwa na backup.

27. Tọọ kọmputa cloud na Amazon na 16 processors na 255 GB RAM (ma ọ bụ kọmputa siri ike ọ bụla nwere ihe dịka 120 GB ma ọ bụ karịa RAM).

28. Wụnye NoMachine na server na client.

29. Wepụta ahịrị/ogologo site na nchekwa cloud gaa na kọmputa cloud.

30. Mepee GIMP na kọmputa cloud.

31. Banye ahịrị/ogologo ọ bụla dị ka layer ọhụrụ na GIMP.

32. Jikọọ ahịrị/ogologo ọ bụla ọnụ site na iji ngwaọrụ na-ebufe na ịgwakọta.

33. Tinye agba azụ dị ka layer.

34. Wepụta foto ikpeazụ dị ka TIFF.

35. Gbanwee foto ikpeazụ na pasent dị ala maka ịlele web.

Nkọwa, ọ bụrụ na foto buru ibu, ya bụ, karịrị 4 GB, ị gaghị enwe ike iwepụta ya dị ka otu ihe na GIMP dị ka TIFF. (Update June 2022: Version kacha ọhụrụ nke GIMP, 2.10.32, na-akwado mbubata na mbupụ BigTIFF, ya mere nzọụkwụ a ugbu a bụ nhọrọ) Ya mere, na GIMP, ị ga-etinye akara nduzi kwụ ọtọ/kwụ ọtọ wee jiri ngwaọrụ guillotine foto wepụta foto ndị nwe onwe ha. Mgbe ahụ, jiri ImageMagick jiri iwu:

convert Image1.tiff Image2.tiff … -append TIFF64:Output.tiff

36. Mepụta foto krpano.

37. Bulite na Gigapan.

Nkọwa, Gigapan anaghị ahụ faịlụ TIFF buru ibu, ya mere jiri ImageMagick gbanwee foto site na TIFF gaa na PSB wee bulite ya.
